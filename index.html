What is Fido Facts?

Fido Facts is designed to allow users to look up dog breeds that they are interested in and learn information on them. Through "The Dog Api" we are able to learn things such as, but not limited to; average life span, temperament, and weight for the breed of your choosing. Throught this we hope to inform users on what they might expect when getting a specific breed of dog.
